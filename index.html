Nike History

This NIKE Impact Report represents the first year of performance against our 2025 Purpose targets. This set of 29 enterprise targets, plus the corporate commitments with a longer time frame – like our Science-Based Carbon Target (2030) and Net Zero Target (2050) – form an aggregated view of NIKE’s long-term goals and public commitments to meet stakeholder expectations and align with NIKE’s business priorities. This report covers NIKE’s fiscal year 2021 (June 1, 2020, through May 31, 2021).
